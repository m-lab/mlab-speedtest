About

Measurement Lab (M-Lab) provides the largest collection of open Internet performance data on the planet. As a consortium of research, industry, and public-interest partners, M-Lab is dedicated to providing an ecosystem for the open, verifiable measurement of global network performance.

Open Source

Measurement tools are openly licensed and operated, allowing third parties to develop their own client-side measurement software and audit the source.

Public Data

Data collected is made available to the public, allowing researchers and anyone else to build on a common pool of network measurement information.

Global

Distributed infrastructure and large user base provides an open, verifiable measurement platform for global network performance.

Trusted

Used by public organization, academic researchers, regulators and companies around the world to understand the health of the Internet.
